I think all the questions provided are very essential questions we should all ask ourselves, both about technology in the normal sense we think about it, but also about information and intention in general. Information can be largely distorted and presented in ways to encourage/discourage us to do certain things or think a certain way. We tend to extract ourselves of responsibility when the responsibility is shared amonst large populations, when we divide that responsibility we convince ourselves that our contributions are minimal, avoiding the responsibility. However, we are responsible for the implications of our actions, even if they are small across a large scale, and we should admit that and acknowledge it. Back
