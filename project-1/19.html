Ibby's

Nestled at the heart of the Washington University in St. Louis campus, Ibby's is located in the Danforth University Center. This unique campus bistro offers fresh, creative cuisine inspired by local ingredients and the seasons. Behind the scenes, Ibby's chefs apply classical cooking techniques to their ingredients to produce cuisine that is stunning to the eye and exciting to the palate. With a focus on sustainable and locally sourced ingredients, Ibby's offers a delightful, fresh menu to satisfy all your dining needs.
